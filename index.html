PIZZA PLACES REVIEWS

Blaze Pizza ★★★★

Blaze Pizza have the best pizza as they have a variety of toppings you can choose from including lactose free options and vegan options. Specifically, the one I love going to is located in Tampa, FL.

Papa Johns Pizza ★★

Papa Johns are striving to improve their catering options on vegan pizza which is cool; however, there is no lactose free cheese pizza. Due to not having that option this place is my least favorite pizzeria to order pizza from.
